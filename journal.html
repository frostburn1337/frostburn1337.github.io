Dear Diary,
So it's been the first week of the New Year. It has been an interesting week I suppose. I've truely delved into Final Fantasy XIV lore and I find myself falling in love with the world. Something I never thought would happen. You know I'm antisocial right? Well they implimented a system that allows you to run the main story with NPCs, thereby making the main story solo friendly. I have also decided to change my character from a Miqo'te to an Au Ra. Which leads me on to the second major thing that happened this week. I learned about Kobolds from D&D. The lore surrounding them is just so awesome. I find them so fascinating and fitting that I changed my fursona to a Kobold. It fits into my reverance of Dragons and wanting to provide for my little community. Well, We'll see where the aether currents take us next.
Till Sea Swallows All,
